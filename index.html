Who We Are

Built for Organisations
That Refuse to
Compromise

Churi Events is a London-based event consultancy built for organisations that refuse to compromise — on quality, on impact, or on results.

We specialise in the delivery of complex, large-scale events across Europe, North America, the Middle East and Asia. From conferences bringing together thousands of attendees to high-stakes summits with hundreds of sponsors and partners, we operate where the pressure is highest and the margin for error is lowest.
What sets us apart is our ability to build and execute from the ground up. Whether you are launching a brand-new event or scaling an existing one, we embed ourselves in your vision and turn it into a seamless operational reality — on time, on budget, and beyond expectations. No templates, no off-the-shelf solutions — just sharp strategic thinking and hands-on execution, tailored to your goals.
